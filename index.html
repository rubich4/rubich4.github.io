The objective of the game is to destroy all the asteroids that apper on the screen, acquiring scores and beating levels.

CENTIPEDE++

This version of the centipede recreates the original 1981 version in current programming language, the gameplay has slight improvements and additional effects but the original essence has been maintained.

The game consists of destroying the 12 parts that make up the centipede in order to advance to the next level, by passing levels, the difficulty will increase and new enemies will appear, including new mechanics.
